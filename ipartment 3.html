Actor situation

The rason why Zhao Ji no longer plays Lin Wanyu in the fourth and fifth seasons is that she had severe symptoms of anemia when filming the third season of "ipartment". In addition to filming the plot day and night, her physical condition has not improved. In the end, she decided to resign from the fifth season of ipartment, and later even quit the showbiz directly.

In the fifth season, Jin Shijia and Wang Chuanjun expressed their refusal to participate, which became the regret of the audience. In fact, the popularity of the show "ipartment" is not all good for the two actors. "ipartment" was once caught in a plagiarism storm, and was picked up by netizens for plagiarizing the American drama "Friends", which touched their bottom line as actors. In addition, both characters are obviously clumsy, funny and stubborn. And under their excellent acting skills, these characters are engraved in the minds of the audience. Instead, these stereotypes have become obstacles in their acting careers, making it difficult for them to escape.

Because of Wang Chuanjun's resignation, Tang Youyou played by Deng Jiajia was also cancelled in the fifth season.
